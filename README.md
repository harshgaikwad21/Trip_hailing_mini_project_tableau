### Service Type Analysis
- Ride-hailing slightly dominates over Taxi in trip count
- Ride-hailing generates **higher total revenue** compared to Taxi

### Payment Behavior
- **UPI and Wallet** are the most frequently used payment methods
- **Cash usage is declining**, indicating digital payment adoption
- Card payments remain stable across trips

### Visuals Used
- Pie chart for Service Type Share

## 3Ô∏è‚É£ Zone & Distance Insights

### Pickup Zones
- **Downtown and Suburb North** have the highest trip pickups
- Airport pickups are comparatively lower but significant

### Drop-off Zones
- **Downtown** is the most common drop-off location
- Business District and Railway Station show high trip density

### Distance vs Fare
- Strong positive correlation between **trip distance and fare amount**
- Ride-hailing trips tend to cover **longer distances with higher fares**

### Visuals Used
- Bar charts for Pickup & Drop-off Zones
- Scatter plot for Distance vs Fare
